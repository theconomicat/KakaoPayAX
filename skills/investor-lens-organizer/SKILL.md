# Investor Lens Organizer

Use this skill after source gathering to organize follow-up research questions.

## Lenses

- Buffett-style Value Lens: moat, durable cash generation, balance sheet, return on capital, valuation discipline.
- Fisher-style Quality Growth Lens: long-term growth, R&D, management quality, product pipeline, market expansion.
- Lynch-style Understandability Lens: simple business story, observable customer behavior, category growth, earnings narrative.
- Risk-first Lens: regulation, cycle, rates, FX, competition, supply chain, accounting quality, governance.

## Rules

- Say "inspired by" or "style lens"; do not impersonate named investors.
- Do not claim what any investor would do.
- Produce questions and source-backed observations, not final investment conclusions.
