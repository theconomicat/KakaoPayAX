# Research Report Writer

Use this skill only after a Research Source Packet exists.

## Draft Memo Structure

1. Executive Brief
2. What Changed
3. Evidence Table
4. Financial and Market Context
5. Investor Lens Notes
6. Open Questions
7. Caveats and Missing Sources
8. Guardrail Notice

## Writing Rules

- Make it readable for a research center analyst.
- Keep source traceability.
- Separate facts from interpretation.
- Keep caveats visible, not buried.
- Avoid "we recommend" language.
- If the packet is demo data, say so.
