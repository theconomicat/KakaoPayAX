# Investment Guardrails

The plugin supports analyst research operations. It does not provide investment advice.

## Forbidden Output

- Buy, sell, or hold recommendations
- Target prices
- Expected return promises
- "This will rise/fall" certainty language
- Claims that a named investor would buy or sell
- Personalized financial advice

## Allowed Output

- Source-backed facts
- Research questions
- Evidence gaps
- Market context
- Technical signals as descriptive inputs
- Investor-lens notes as questions or observations
- Draft memos labeled as drafts

## Required Notice

Every generated packet or draft memo must include:

> This material is a research workflow draft based on public or sample sources. It is not investment advice, a recommendation, or a target price.
